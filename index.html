Privacy Policy for Rhythm Chess

Last updated: June 2025

We respect your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used in our mobile game Rhythm Chess.

1. What We Collect

We use Unity Analytics to collect anonymous gameplay data, such as:

  • How many levels (“leaves”) you have completed
  • How many attempts you needed to beat a level

We do not collect personally identifiable information like your name, email address, or location.

2. Why We Collect This Data

We collect gameplay data to:

  • Understand how players engage with the game
  • Balance the difficulty
  • Improve future updates

3. Who Collects the Data

Analytics data is collected by Unity Technologies, the provider of Unity Analytics. Unity may collect device-related information such as device model, operating system version, and anonymized identifiers.

You can find Unity’s privacy policy here: https://unity.com/legal/game-player-and-app-user-privacy-policy

4. Children’s Privacy

Rhythm Chess is not directed at children under 13. Unity Analytics is configured to comply with COPPA and other child data protection laws.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You may disable analytics tracking in the game’s settings (if available).
You may also contact us at rhythmrook.app@gmail.com to request information about your data or ask for its deletion.
